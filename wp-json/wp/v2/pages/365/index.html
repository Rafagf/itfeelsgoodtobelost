Welcome to our site! A place where we, Rafa and Iga, share our love for travel and the joy of getting lost.<\/p>\n

Rafa<\/h2>\n

Born in Spain, Rafa is a software engineer specializing in mobile apps. Beyond his professional life, he’s a passionate traveler, an avid sports enthusiast who loves playing tennis and football, a movie buff, and a BIG-TIME dog lover. His sense of adventure and curiosity has led him to explore the world, one country at a time.<\/p>\n

Iga<\/h2>\n

Iga is a Polish anthropologist and market researcher, she has a deep fascination with different cultures and societies. Her hobbies include photography, yoga, cinema, and of course, traveling. Her anthropological background and love for photography bring a unique perspective to our travel experiences, capturing the essence of the places we visit.<\/p>\n

Our Journey<\/h2>\n

We met in 2009 when Rafa moved to Poland to study abroad and have been inseparable since then.
\nOur traveling journey really began when we decided to take a leap of faith and travel long-term in Asia. This experience changed our lives, opening our eyes to the beauty of getting lost and finding ourselves in the process. Today, we continue to travel to different countries, embracing the feeling of being lost and using it as a compass to guide us to new adventures.<\/p>\n

Our Mission<\/h2>\n

Our mission is to inspire you to embrace the unknown and find joy in the journey of discovery. We aim to share our experiences, tips, and insights to help you make the most of your travels. Whether you’re a seasoned traveler or just starting your journey, we hope our stories will inspire you to see the world through a new lens.<\/p>\n
