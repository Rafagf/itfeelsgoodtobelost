Have you heard of the famous Shibuya crossing in Tokyo?<\/h3>\n

The Shibuya crossing in Tokyo is rumoured to be the busiest intersection in the world. With more than 3000 people crossing it at peak times and coming from all directions at once, this place is any photographer’s dream. This is why it has become one of the most popular sightseeing attractions in Tokyo, especially at night, when the flashing lights make it simply special.<\/p>\n

The Shibuya crossing in Tokyo has appeared in many movies, such as Lost in Translation, The Fast and the Furious or Resident Evil.<\/p><\/blockquote>\n

A Japanese friend told me once that if you go to Tokyo and don’t cross it at least a few times you can’t really say you’ve been to Tokyo. I have to admit it looks hectic from outside,\u00a0but from inside it feels really natural. It’s just one of many examples of how polite and organised is the Japanese culture and its people.<\/p>\n
